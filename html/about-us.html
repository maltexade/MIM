Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming Nigeria's leading weighing solutions provider

Where It All Began

Founded in 2003 by Engineer Michael Ibrahim, MIM Electronics Enterprise started as a small weighing equipment retailer in Lagos. Driven by a vision to provide accurate, reliable weighing solutions to Nigerian businesses, we began with just three employees and a commitment to excellence.

What started as a passion for precision engineering has grown into Nigeria's most trusted name in industrial weighing solutions. Today, we serve over 500 clients across various industries, from small retail businesses to large manufacturing companies.

"Every scale we install, every service we provide, carries our commitment to helping Nigerian businesses grow with precision and confidence."
